About Her's

The story of the band "Her's" is tragic. Before I was aware of the fate that befell the group, I often wondered why the group had not produced new music in a while. After all, their dreamy voices and melodious beats serve as a perfect backdrop for any setting.

It wasn't long before I finally decided to hunt for information. The members of the two-person band were killed while on tour in the U.S., therefore halting any new album creation - forever.

The pair met in Liverpool while attending University. Stephen Fitzpatrick and Audun Laading employed a symphony of sound that I have instituted as the perfect nighttime playlist at the brewery I work at.

Discography

Due to their untimely death(s), Her's was only able to record two albums: Invitation to Her's (2018) and Songs of Her's (2017).
